2. Scenario planning / what-if comparisons
3. Monte Carlo probability of success
4. Tax-aware planning and optimization
5. Income source modeling (state/social security, pension, annuity)
6. Visual dashboards and plan summaries
7. Advanced decumulation and stress testing in higher tiers

## Consumer-facing differentiators seen in market

- Strong visual storytelling for trade-offs
- Quick, low-friction onboarding
- Action-oriented recommendations, not only charts
