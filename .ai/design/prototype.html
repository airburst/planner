Recommendation rationale

Recommended approach Use part of the available SIPP tax-free cash during the bridge years to reduce taxable drawdown.
Why it helps This keeps more annual income inside lower tax bands while preserving some ISA flexibility.
Trade-off Using tax-free cash earlier reduces the remaining tax-free entitlement available later in retirement.
